  • Write down how an ATM works
    • An ATM works by taking a person's card into the machine asking for
      a PIN of the user then types into the ATM. After the number is
      verified the user is asked questions like: What account do they want
      to use? Do you want to deposit money or take it out? After the user
      selects the option that they want the task is performed and the ATM
      then asks whether or not the user has any more tasks they wish to
      perform. If not, the card and a receipt are spit out of the machine
      and the transaction is over. The machine is in turn communicating
      with the banks' data about who is using the machine how much
      money they have they have and what they want to do with it.
